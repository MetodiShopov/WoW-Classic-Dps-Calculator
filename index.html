How is it calculated:

Rotation used: Arcane Power on cool down and casts only Frostbolt Rank 10 at level 60.

The calculation creates a character using the given attributes.

Then simulates a fight against the chosen enemy.

The simulation ends when the character has no more mana to cast Frostbolt. The sequence is repeated 500 times and prints the average numbers.
